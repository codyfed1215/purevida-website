Your Filters & Maintenance

Every PureVida system uses industrial-grade filtration designed for heavy commercial use. These filters are built to last -- but to ensure your water stays at the highest quality, we replace them at least once a year. You never have to think about it.

PureVida is full service. Your monthly rate covers everything:

Professional installation and setup at your location

Scheduled filter replacements -- at least annually

All maintenance, repairs, and parts

System sanitization and cleaning

No surprise fees -- one predictable monthly rate, that's it
