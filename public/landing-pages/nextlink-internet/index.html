The 5-Gallon Jug Hassle Ends Here

If your office still relies on 5-gallon jugs, you already know the headaches.

Waiting on Deliveries

Delivery schedules don't revolve around your office. Missed or delayed drop-offs leave your team without water when they need it most.

Running Out of Water

When the last jug runs dry between deliveries, your team is stuck. No water until the next scheduled drop-off — that's unacceptable.

Jugs Taking Up Space

Stacks of full and empty 5-gallon jugs cluttering your break room, hallway, or storage closet. That's valuable space your office needs.

Employee Injury Liability

A 5-gallon jug weighs over 40 lbs. Every time an employee lifts one onto the cooler, you're risking a back injury and a workers' comp claim.

Coordinating Pickups

Empty jugs pile up and someone has to arrange a pickup or haul them out. It's an ongoing chore nobody wants to deal with.

Unpredictable Costs

Between delivery fees, bottle deposits, rental charges, and fuel surcharges, you never know what next month's water bill will look like.
