## PureVida's 6-Stage Filtration

Every system runs the same 6-stage process:

- **Sediment Filter** — removes dirt, rust, silt, and sand
- **Carbon Block Filter** — removes chlorine, chemicals, herbicides, pesticides, and VOCs
- **Reverse Osmosis Membrane** — filters down to 0.0001 micron, removing heavy metals, PFAs, microplastics, pharmaceuticals, and dissolved solids
- **Granular Activated Carbon Filter** — polishes the water for taste and removes residual organics
- **Boost Filter (Remineralization)** — adds back essential minerals and electrolytes (Potassium, Sodium, Calcium, Magnesium) while increasing the pH (standard on select systems, optional upgrade on others)
- **Auto Active Purification** — UV Light or Ozonation with Activated Oxygen runs continuously to keep both the water AND the system tank pristine

Stage 6 is the one that matters most in clinical settings. It's not a bolt-on "self-cleaning feature" — it IS the final purification stage. Continuous, automatic, no scheduled cleaning.
