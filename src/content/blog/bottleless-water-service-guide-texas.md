## How Does the Filtration Work?

Every PureVida system uses advanced, 6-stage filtration that purifies your water right at the point of dispensing. Here's what each stage does:

1. **Sediment filtration** — removes dirt, rust, and particulate matter
2. **Carbon block filtration** — removes chlorine, taste, and odor compounds
3. **Reverse osmosis membrane** — filters down to 0.0001 microns, removing [PFAS](/blog/pfas-texas-drinking-water), heavy metals, and dissolved contaminants
4. **Granular activated carbon** — polishes the water and removes any residual taste or odor
5. **Remineralization and pH boost** — adds back beneficial minerals and balances pH for better taste
6. **Activated oxygen (ozonation) or UV light** — provides final-stage disinfection and prevents bacterial growth

The result is water that's cleaner, healthier, and better tasting than anything from a bottle. Most customers notice the difference from day one.

Our service technicians handle the entire installation, and it's simpler than most people expect.

We use push-to-connect fittings and quarter-inch polyethylene tubing that runs from the back of the system to the closest water source — typically a kitchen or breakroom sink. The water source doesn't have to be right next to the unit.
