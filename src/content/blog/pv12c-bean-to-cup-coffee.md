## What makes the PV12c different

The PV12c is a true bean-to-cup machine. That phrase matters. Most office "coffee solutions" pre-grind the beans, vacuum-seal them in pods or cartridges, and ship them to you weeks (sometimes months) after roasting. The PV12c does the opposite — whole beans sit in the canister until the moment your employee presses a button. Then they're ground, fresh, per drink. The result is the cup of coffee you remember from a real café, not the cup your office has settled for.

Two separate bean canisters mean a dark roast and a light roast can run side by side. Your team picks the roast they want, and the machine handles the rest.

Any combination of coffee, chocolate, vanilla, and milk topping is on the menu — lattes, mochas, vanilla cappuccinos, hot chocolates, americanos, plain black coffee, hot water for tea. All from one machine. All built on demand.

A touch-screen display makes the menu obvious — your team isn't memorizing button combos.

Remote usage monitoring means whoever runs the office can see consumption patterns without standing next to the machine. Helpful for offices that want to budget bean and powder orders accurately.
